01 / About

A builder who loves a clean dataset more than a clever one-liner.

Most of my learning happens in Jupyter notebooks — loading a real dataset, cleaning it patiently, and letting the exploratory data analysis do the talking before a model ever enters the room. That habit started during my Data Science internship at Launched Edu and hasn't let go.

I'm also drawn to the other side of the stack: during my Cybersecurity internship at Elmer Technologies, I sat with breach logs, hunted anomalies, and wrote risk notes. That duality — analytics & security — is how I think about a problem: what's there, and what shouldn't be.

Off the keyboard, I play for the Mahindra University football team, make work with the Kalakriti art club, and volunteer for the Semaine de la Francophonie each year at Etincelle.
